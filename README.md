## 📖 About

**PetConnectta** is a web platform designed to bridge the gap between pets in need and the people who want to help — whether through **adoption**, **donation**, or simply **raising awareness**. Built with a deep love for animals and community, this project was born out of a desire to make pet adoption more accessible, human, and digital across Brazil.

With an intuitive, welcoming interface, PetConnectta makes it easier than ever to find your new best friend or support local shelters and animals in need.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🐶 **Browse Pets for Adoption** — View pets available for adoption with photos and descriptions  
- 📍 **Location-Based Discovery** — Find animals near you  
- 📝 **Adoption Requests** — Submit interest directly through the platform  
- 🏠 **Shelter Profiles** — Shelters and NGOs can register and manage their animals  
- 💬 **Contact & Connection** — Direct communication between adopters and caretakers  
- 📱 **Responsive Design** — Fully optimized for mobile and desktop

## 🌎 Why Portuguese?

PetConnectta was conceived and developed **specifically for the Brazilian market**. The entire user-facing interface, forms, and content are in **pt-BR (Brazilian Portuguese)** to best serve the local community of animal lovers, NGOs, and adopters across the country.
